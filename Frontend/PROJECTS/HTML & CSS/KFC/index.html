Lickin Good.

KFC, or Kentucky Fried Chicken, is an internationally renowned fast-food chain that has left an indelible mark on the culinary world. Founded by Colonel Harland Sanders in 1930, KFC has become synonymous with mouthwatering fried chicken, cooked to perfection with a secret blend of 11 herbs and spices. With its crispy, golden-brown crust and tender, juicy meat, KFC's fried chicken has captured the hearts and taste buds of people in over 145 countries.
