SEMICONDUCTORS

Semiconductors is defined as a substance whose resistivity lies between insulator and conductor

Semiconductors has negative temperature coefficient means when we increase the temperature the resistivity decrease and when we decrease the temperature resistivity increases.

CLASSIFICATION OF SEMICONDUCTORS

Semiconductord can be classified into two category
1.INTRINCI SEMSICONDUCTORS
2.EXTRINCIC SEMICONDUCTORS

1. INTRINSIC SEMICONDUCTOR

Semiconductors in its extremly pure form is known as intrinsic semiconductors

2.EXTRINSIC SEMICONDUCTOR

An impure semiconductor which is formed by doping a pure semiconductor is called as an extrinsic semiconductor

Extrinsic semiconductor are of two types
Pentavalent
Trivalent

1.Pentavalent-Those element which has five valance electron in there outermost cell are called pentavalent
in pentavalent, electrons are in majority and the current

2.Trivalent- those elemement which has three valance electron in there outer most cell are called trivalent
in trivalent holes are in majority
